#LockBox>Ekifo kyo eky'obukuumi bwa vidiyo ekikuumibwa

Leero, ng’okusasula ebirimu, okusomesa ku yintaneeti n’obuyinza bw’okukozesa mu ngeri ya digito byeyongera okukula, okusaasaanya ebirimu ku vidiyo mu ngeri ey’obukuumi kifuuse kyetaagisa.

**LockBox ye nkola ya encrypted player n'okuddukanya eddembe eyazimbibwa mu ngeri ey'enjawulo ku vidiyo ez'omuwendo omungi. ** **

Si muzannyi yekka, wabula era ekibinja ekijjuvu ekya "obukuumi bw'ebirimu n'ebikozesebwa mu kukola ssente mu by'obusuubuzi".

### Endagiriro y'okuwanula: https://lockbox.movingshop.cn/en/

---

## 1. Vidiyo elabe “abo abalina okugiraba” bokka .

Ofunye ebizibu bino:

- Akatambi k’omusomo kakwatibwa ku screen ne kasaasaanyizibwa
- Ebirimu ebisasulwa biweerezeddwa ne bifuluma
- Fayiro eno yawanulibwa mu ngeri emenya amateeka
- Abakozesa abalala abatali bakozesa abakkirizibwa nabo basobola okuyingira

Ekigendererwa kya LockBox kyangu nnyo:

> **Bulijjo kuzannya vidiyo mu kifo ekikkirizibwa kyokka. ** **

Okuyita mu kufuga okuzannya okw’emitendera mingi n’enkola z’okukakasa embeera, okubunyisa ebirimu bulijjo kufugibwa.

---

## 2. Enkola z’obukuumi eziwera okuzimba enkola y’obukuumi bw’okuzannya

LockBox ekwata enkola y’okukuuma obukuumi ey’enjawulo:

- Enkola y'okuzuula ebiwandiiko ebiziyiza screen
- Okuziyiza ekyuma ekirabika ng'ekola okuzuula embeera
- Okuziyiza okulongoosa n'okukuuma empiso ya koodi
- Okukebera obulungi bw'embeera y'okuzannya
- Enkola ey’okukakasa olukusa ekyukakyuka

Obusobozi buno bwegatta ne bukola obukuumi ku ddaala ly’enkola:

> **Si nsonga emu ey'obukuumi, wabula nkola ya bukuumi okutwaliza awamu. ** **

---

## 3. Enkola y’olukusa ekyukakyuka, ekyukakyuka okusinziira ku nkola za bizinensi eziwera

Oba oli mutonzi ssekinnoomu oba omukozesa ow’ekitongole, LockBox esobola okukyusakyusa mu nsonga za bizinensi yo:

- ⏱ Olukusa okusinziira ku budde (ennaku/emyezi/emyaka)
- 🔢 Olukusa buli kiseera
- 📱 Olukusa lw'okusiba ekyuma
- 👑 Enkola y'omutendera gw'obwammemba
- 🔄 Okutereeza olukusa olukyukakyuka

Osobola okugatta amateeka mu ddembe n’okuzimba enkola yo ey’okusasula ebirimu.

---

## 4. Okugatta obwammemba n’okuddukanya ebirimu

LockBox si muzannyi yekka, wabula enkola y’emirimu enzijuvu:

- Enzirukanya y'enkola y'obwammemba bw'abakozesa
- Okufuga mu kiseera ekituufu embeera y’olukusa
- Ebibalo by’ebiwandiiko by’okuyingira kw’abakozesa
- Okusaasaanya ebirimu n'okusengeka olukusa
- Olukusa lwa batch n'obusobozi bw'okuddukanya

Leka olongoose okuva ku "omugabi w'ebirimu" okudda ku "omukozi w'ebirimu".

---

## 5. Obuwagizi bwa mikutu mingi, zannye essaawa yonna ne wonna

LockBox ewagira embeera z'emirimu ezirina terminal eziwera era ekwata ku mbeera z'okukozesa enkulu:

- Ebyuma ebikozesebwa ku ssimu
- Enkola ya Desktop
- Embeera y'okuzannya ku yintaneeti ku mukutu gwa yintaneeti
- Embeera y'okuteeka mu nkola intranet y'ebitongole

Bw’omala okuyingira, terminal eziwera zibeerawo.

---

## 6. Okuteekawo enkola y’obukuumi ey’ekiseera ekiwanvu ku muwendo gw’ebirimu

Mu mulembe guno ng’ebirimu bisobola okukoppololwa mu ngeri ennyangu, omugaso ogwa nnamaddala guli mu “kufuga.”

Endowooza enkulu eya LockBox eri nti:

> **Leka ebirimu bikozeseddwa naye nga tebisaasaanyiziddwa nga tebifugibwa. ** **

Yamba abayiiya, amatendekero g’ebyenjigiriza n’ebitongole okukyusa ebirimu okufuuka eby’obugagga bya digito ebiwangaala.

Bw’oba oli:

- Okuddukanya emisomo egisasulwa
- Okuzimba enkola y’ebintu ebikozesebwa mu kumanya
- Okukola emisomo egy’omunda mu kkampuni
- oba okukuuma eby'obugagga bya vidiyo eby'omuwendo omungi
